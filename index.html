The Websites I Like

  • Amazon - Amazon is a huge buisness today. I like their website because the homepage is clean and the site is so easy to navigate. You can type what you're looking for and you can scroll through multiple pages of product to find what youre looking for.
  • Youtube - Youtube has a very welcoming home page. The home page gives you lots of content based on the popularity of videos.
  • Apple - Apple has a website that is very clean and aethstically pleasing. I am a huge fan of Apple and I enjoy their products.
